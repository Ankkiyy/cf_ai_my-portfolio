## ❌ CRITICAL AVOID LIST

### Never Do (Black-Hat/Spam)
- ❌ Buy backlinks or use link farms
- ❌ Keyword stuffing (>3% density)
- ❌ Private Blog Networks (PBNs)
- ❌ Automated link building tools
- ❌ Cloaking (showing different content to Google)
- ❌ Hidden text/links
- ❌ Comment spam with links
- ❌ Translate content (low quality)
- ❌ User redirect scheme
- ❌ Doorway pages

### Avoid (Risk Zones)
- ⚠️ Exact match anchor text (>10% of links)
- ⚠️ Unnatural anchor text distribution
- ⚠️ Link exchanges/reciprocal linking
- ⚠️ Paid links (without rel="sponsored")
- ⚠️ Footer links on directories
- ⚠️ Nofollow-to-follow conversion
- ⚠️ Sudden traffic spikes (might trigger review)
